Hi Product Hunt! I built LogPulse AI because I couldn't find a SIEM that had AI analysis without being expensive, complex, or privacy-invasive.

Three things that make it different:

1. **Privacy-first** — 11 PII categories (IPs, emails, credentials, etc.) are filtered *before* any data reaches the LLM. Plus you can use self-hosted models (Ollama) for fully air-gapped setups.

2. **Actually affordable** — 16 optimization techniques reduce LLM costs to $2-10/month. Template deduplication alone eliminates ~90% of redundant API calls.

3. **5-minute Docker deploy** with full GUI configuration. No YAML, no SSH, no Kubernetes.

It's MIT licensed, non-commercial, and free forever. I'm the sole developer and happy to answer any questions!
